It's the core of Ignite! It's a living profile created from your onboarding interview that captures your unique voice, expertise, stories, and goals, which our AI uses to generate authentic content.

Our drafts are designed to be a powerful starting point, not a finished product. You have full control to edit, refine, and ensure every piece of content perfectly reflects you. The goal is to save you 80% of the effort.

Ignite was born from a simple observation: the world is full of brilliant people who are invisible.

My name is Yasir Kamran...

...and for years, I watched incredible colleagues, friends, and entrepreneurs struggle with the same paradox. They were experts in their fieldsengineers who could build marvels, marketers who could grow companies, leaders who could inspire teamsbut when it came to building their own personal brand, they were paralyzed.

They knew they should be sharing their knowledge, but they were held back by a silent trio of fears: the fear of the blank page, the fear of sounding inauthentic, and the fear of shouting into the void.

They were trying to compete in the creator economy with a fragmented toolkit and a drained battery. They had the desire, but not the system. The expertise, but not the execution.

I knew there had to be a better way.

What if we could build a tool that acted less like a content machine and more like a trusted strategic partner? A platform that didn't just generate words, but amplified an individual's true voice?
